### 1.2 Qualification Criteria

**Green Light (proceed to call):**
- Budget aligns ($7,500+)
- Clear pain points identified
- Decision-maker engaged
- Timeline reasonable
- Technical baseline adequate

**Yellow Light (needs clarification):**
- Budget unclear
- Vague goals
- Multiple stakeholders unnamed
- Very aggressive timeline

**Red Flag (likely not a fit):**
- Budget under $5,000
- Expects AI to replace entire workforce
- No technical baseline and unwilling to learn
- Unrealistic expectations ("fully autonomous in 1 week")

### 1.3 Initial Call Script (15-30 min)

```markdown
## Pre-Call Prep
- Review intake form
- Research company website
- Note 2-3 specific questions based on their responses

## Call Structure

### Opening (2 min)
"Thanks for taking the time, [Name]. I reviewed your intake form and I'm excited to learn more about [Company]. Before we dive in, is there anything that's changed since you submitted that form?"

### Discovery (10-15 min)
1. "Walk me through a typical day/week - where do you feel like you're wasting the most time?"

2. "You mentioned [pain point from form] - can you give me a specific example of when that cost you time or money?"

3. "What have you already tried to solve this?"

4. "If we could wave a magic wand and fix one thing, what would have the biggest impact on your business?"

5. "Who else would be involved in this decision? Who needs to be trained?"

### Education (5 min)
"Let me quickly explain how Launchpad Pro works..."
- Explain LAUNCH Method (30 seconds each phase)
- Timeline expectations (2-4 weeks)
- What's included vs what's not
- Post-launch support

### Qualifying (3 min)
1. "Based on what you've described, this sounds like a [tier] engagement. Does a budget of $X-$Y align with what you had in mind?"

2. "What's your ideal start date?"

3. "Is there anyone else who needs to be part of the next conversation?"

### Close (2 min)
**If qualified:**
"Great. Here's what I suggest as next steps:
1. I'll send you a detailed proposal within 48 hours
2. We schedule a 60-minute discovery session to map your current state
3. Upon approval, we kick off within [X days]

Does that work for you?"

**If not qualified:**
"Based on what you've shared, I think Launchpad Academy might be a better fit for where you are right now. It's self-paced, costs $997, and you'd learn everything we'd implement for you. Would you like me to send you more info on that?"
```
