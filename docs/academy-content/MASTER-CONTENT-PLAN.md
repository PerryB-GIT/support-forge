- Bullet points over walls of text

## Quality Standards

### Video Scripts
- Natural speech patterns (contractions, pauses)
- [PAUSE] markers for pacing
- [SCREEN: description] for visual cues
- Pronunciation guides for technical terms
- 150-170 words per minute pacing
- Conversational but professional tone

### Written Guides
- Step numbered clearly (Step 1, Step 2...)
- Screenshots described with [IMAGE: description]
- Expected outputs shown
- Common errors and fixes included
- Time estimates per section

### Templates
- Pre-filled example data
- Clear instructions in document
- Branded with Support Forge colors
- Editable formats (not PDF-only)
