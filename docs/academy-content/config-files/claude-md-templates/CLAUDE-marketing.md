## Content Types & Templates

### Social Media Posts
**LinkedIn Format:**
```
[Hook - first line that stops scroll]

[2-3 short paragraphs with value]

[Call to action]

#hashtag1 #hashtag2 #hashtag3
```

**Instagram Caption Format:**
```
[Attention-grabbing first line]

[Story or value proposition]

[Call to action with emoji]

.
.
.
#hashtags (in comments or at bottom)
```

### Email Templates
**Newsletter Structure:**
1. Subject line (under 50 chars)
2. Preview text
3. Header/Hero section
4. Main content (3 sections max)
5. CTA button
6. Footer with social links

**Subject Line Formulas:**
- How to [achieve result] in [timeframe]
- [Number] ways to [solve problem]
- The [adjective] guide to [topic]
- Why [common belief] is wrong

### Blog Post Structure
1. SEO-optimized title (60 chars max)
2. Meta description (155 chars)
3. Introduction with hook
4. H2 subheadings every 300 words
5. Bullet points and numbered lists
6. Internal links (2-3)
7. External links (1-2 authoritative)
8. CTA at end
9. FAQ section (for featured snippets)
